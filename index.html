Comparative Analysis of Deep Reinforcement Learning Algorithms (Undergraduate Thesis)

The primary objective of this project is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of advanced reinforcement learning algorithms, like Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient (DDPG), Soft-Actor Critic (SAC) and Twin Delayed Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient (TD3), for suppressing vibration in a rotary flexible link system.

📄 View Thesis Report

Arm Motion Planning of Humanoid Robots Research

The research is conducted to obtain motion planning algorithm of humanoid robots. Our aim is to create the motion of the arm of humanoid robot automatically so that it can avoid obstacles in its surroundings. Our system will use stereo vision to obtain the map of the environment and using mesh modeling, the accumulation of vision input continuously will be done. We will implement PRM (Probabilistic Roadmap) to plan the motion of the robot arm.
